Exhibition 1: Primate Perspectives

This exhibition explores how artists interpret the intelligence, curiosity, and social behavior of monkeys. Through paintings, sculptures, and digital media, the collection highlights parallels between human creativity and primate problem-solving, encouraging visitors to see the world from a different perspective.

Exhibition 2: Monkey Business: Art and Mischief

This exhibition focuses on the playful and chaotic side of creativity. Featuring humorous installations and imaginative artwork inspired by monkeys’ energetic behavior, the exhibit examines how spontaneity, experimentation, and a little mischief can lead to unexpected artistic innovation.
