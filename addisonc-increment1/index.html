MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring creativity through the playful lens of monkeys and primate-inspired art. Through engaging exhibitions, interactive displays, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how curiosity, experimentation, and a little monkey mischief can shape artistic expression. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibits offer fresh perspectives on creativity, nature, and the connections between humans and our primate relatives. Plan your visit to experience imaginative exhibitions, hands-on activities, and events designed to spark curiosity and creativity.
